## Geplante Abschnitte

- Installation (Homebrew, apt, `go install`)
- `lodestone init` — `.lodestone.yaml` anlegen
- `lodestone fingerprint` — Repo-Scan
- `lodestone ingest --source <name>` — Signale sammeln
- `lodestone score` — Empfehlungen erzeugen
- `lodestone signals --top N --since DATE` — Signale filtern
- Konfiguration: Goals, TechInterests, Anti-Hype-Defaults
- Lokale Artefakte unter `.lodestone/`
- Troubleshooting
